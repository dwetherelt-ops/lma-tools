# Like Minds Community Structure

Core community spaces for likemindscommunity.org (Circle.so in Phase 1).

Navigation order:
Start Here > Daily Check-In > Burnout & Energy > Understanding Your Brain > Systems That Work > Shared Experiences > Research & Ideas

---

## 1. Start Here

**Purpose:** Onboarding and orientation.

**Description:** Welcome to Like Minds. This is a good place to begin. Take your time, look around, and join in when you're ready.

**Topics:**
- Introduce yourself (only if you want)
- How the community works
- Where to begin
- Community guidelines

**Pinned post title:** Welcome to Like Minds
**Pinned post body:**
You found us. Welcome.

This community is for neurodivergent adults who are navigating burnout, masking, and systems that were not built for them.

There is no pressure to post. You can read quietly, participate when you want, and move at your own pace.

A few places to start:
- Daily Check-In: share how you are doing today
- Burnout & Energy: the core shared struggle
- Understanding Your Brain: learn how your brain actually works
- Shared Experiences: stories from people who get it

You belong here.

---

## 2. Daily Check-In

**Purpose:** Low-energy participation. The heartbeat of the community.

**Description:** A simple place to check in with the community. Share how you are doing, something small that helped today, or just let people know you are here.

**Topics:**
- How are you doing today?
- One small win
- Something that helped today
- Feeling burned out

**Pinned post title:** Daily Check-In
**Pinned post body:**
How are you doing today?

You can share a small win, something that was hard, or simply say hello.

No pressure. No expectations. Just checking in.

---

## 3. Burnout & Energy

**Purpose:** The core shared struggle. Most ND adults will spend a lot of time here.

**Description:** Talk about exhaustion, energy management, and what burnout actually looks like for neurodivergent adults. This is a space to be honest about how hard it can be.

**Topics:**
- Exhaustion cycles
- Masking fatigue
- Sensory overload
- Work burnout
- Recovery strategies

**Pinned post title:** What does burnout look like for you?
**Pinned post body:**
Burnout hits neurodivergent adults differently. It is not just being tired. It can feel like your brain has stopped cooperating entirely.

What does burnout look like in your life? How do you know when you are in it?

Share as much or as little as you want.

---

## 4. Understanding Your Brain

**Purpose:** Education without being clinical. Aligns with the science-backed positioning.

**Description:** Learn how your brain actually works. ADHD, autism, genetics, dopamine, executive function. Science explained in plain language.

**Topics:**
- ADHD traits
- Autism traits
- Dopamine cycles
- Executive function
- Genetics and ND science

**Pinned post title:** What do you wish you had understood sooner?
**Pinned post body:**
Many of us spent years not understanding why our brains work the way they do.

What is one thing you have learned about your neurodivergence that changed how you see yourself?

---

## 5. Systems That Work

**Purpose:** Practical tools. These posts tend to get high engagement.

**Description:** Routines, tools, and strategies that actually work for neurodivergent brains. No productivity guilt. Just things that help.

**Topics:**
- Routines that work for ND brains
- Productivity without burnout
- Energy management
- Environment design
- Helpful apps and tools

**Pinned post title:** What is one thing that actually works for you?
**Pinned post body:**
Forget the generic advice. What is one strategy, tool, or routine that genuinely helps you manage your day?

It can be small. The small things often matter the most.

---

## 6. Shared Experiences

**Purpose:** Validation and storytelling. Builds deep belonging.

**Description:** Stories from people who get it. Late diagnosis, masking, workplace struggles, relationships, and everything in between. You are not alone in this.

**Topics:**
- Late diagnosis
- Masking stories
- Workplace struggles
- Relationships
- Childhood experiences

**Pinned post title:** When did you first realize your brain worked differently?
**Pinned post body:**
For some of us it was a diagnosis. For others it was a moment of recognition, reading something online, or hearing someone describe exactly how we feel.

When did it click for you?

---

## 7. Research & Ideas

**Purpose:** Attract the science-minded crowd. Supports scientific credibility.

**Description:** New studies, neuroscience, genetics research, and ideas worth discussing. For people who want to go deeper.

**Topics:**
- New studies
- ND genetics
- Neuroscience
- Burnout research
- Discussion of ideas

**Pinned post title:** What research has changed how you think about neurodivergence?
**Pinned post body:**
Like Minds Alliance is built on science. Share a study, article, or finding that shifted your understanding of how neurodivergent brains work.

Link it if you can. We will discuss it together.

---

## Setup Notes

**Circle.so configuration:**
- Create spaces in the navigation order listed above
- Pin the starter post in each space
- Set all spaces to open posting for all members
- Enable threading and replies
- Keep space descriptions short and visible
- Add a gentle note in community guidelines that participation is always optional

**Phase 2 migration:**
When building custom community features in apps/community, use this structure as the seed data for community spaces. The space names, descriptions, and pinned posts can be imported directly.
